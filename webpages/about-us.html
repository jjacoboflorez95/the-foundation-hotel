OUR STORY

Founded in 1985, in Vancouver, British Columbia by Joseph Mills with the goal to bring people a place that they can call home, where they will find a very kind staff, the best attention, wonderful rooms and stunning public areas.

With the pass of years Foundation grew with the help of our customer and now we can offer our services in several places and no matter where you decide to visit us, you will have a very good experience with us!
